# Beast-Mode Brief

## Opportunity Gap
The current market for productivity and SaaS tools is saturated, but there is still a gap for a tool that can help users achieve a state of high productivity and focus, also known as "Beast Mode".

## MAP Features
The tool will have the following features:
* A user-friendly interface that helps users set and achieve their goals
* A system for tracking progress and providing feedback
* A community feature that allows users to connect with others who are also working towards their goals

## Target Persona
The target persona for this tool is individuals who are looking to increase their productivity and achieve their goals. This could include entrepreneurs, freelancers, and students.

## Differentiation Moat
The tool will differentiate itself from other productivity tools by focusing specifically on helping users achieve a state of "Beast Mode". This will be achieved through a combination of features such as goal-setting, progress tracking, and community support.

## North Star Metric
The north star metric for this tool will be the number of users who achieve their goals and report feeling more productive and focused.
